Medication adherence solutions that put people first

Some patients don’t know how to take their medication or simply forget to take it at the prescribed time. Solving these challenges with people-first solutions will help support better outcomes.

Helping Patients

Medication non-adherence accounts for 10 percent of all hospital admissions and results in 125,000 deaths annually1

  • Data-driven insights strengthen existing patient engagement, making addressing non-adherence more accessible
  • Adherence increased 25-30% through medication adherence services2
  • 19% more adherent to receiving their medication through prescription decision support2

Patient Impact

Patients are empowered with the most accurate prescription benefit details, improving their experience, connecting them to alternative support and increasing medication adherence.
