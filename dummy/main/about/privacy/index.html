Privacy Center
CoverMyMeds functions as a Business Associate to a network primarily comprised of Covered Entities (e.g., healthcare providers, pharmacies, pharmacy benefit managers, and health plans) and maintains Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) as required by HIPAA. CoverMyMeds has established technical, physical, and administrative safeguards to comply with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996 (“HIPAA”) Privacy and Security Rule, along with other applicable laws and regulations. CoverMyMeds takes the privacy and security of Protected Health Information (“PHI”) very seriously. For more information about how CoverMyMeds protects PHI and its infrastructure please contact privacy@covermymeds.com.
